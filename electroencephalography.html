The Word “electroencephalography”

A Vocabulary and Meaning

Etymology

It literally means “recording the electrical activity of the brain.”

Definition

Electroencephalography is a noninvasive medical technique used to record and analyze the electrical activity of the brain using electrodes placed on the scalp. .

Denotation

The denotation is the literal, objective meaning:

  • A medical and scientific procedure for measuring brain electrical activity.
Example (Denotation):
Electroencephalography is commonly used to monitor brain activity in patients with seizure disorders.

Connotation

The connotation refers to the ideas or feelings associated with the word:

  • Scientific
  • Advanced medical technology
Example (Connotation):
The use of electroencephalography reassured the family that sophisticated medical technology was guiding the diagnosis.
